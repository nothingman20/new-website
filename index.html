Mind Man

About

Born with blindness, as well as being confined in a wheelchair, young Isaiah Thompson had never had a very interesting life. That all changed, however, when he discovered that his powers were far greater than any mortal man. He has the powers of gods, and the magic of beings unknown. However, he is severely underestimated by any enemy he has faced. Do not underestimate him.

Isaiah in his spirit form

Powers

  • Is able to turn into a green spirit that can travel anywhere at the blink of an instant
  • Is able to physically manipulate the objects around him
  • Is able to manipulate the elements (Earth, Fire, Water, Air)
  • Can also appear as human and animal form in any way he wants

Associates

  • Matt, his carer. He is also his best friend and the one who helped him discover his abilities.
  • Betty "Bee" Owens, a reporter whose mission is to make Mind Man famous around the world. She has teamed up with Isaiah many times in order to face catastrophic threats.
  • The Roughstreet Boys, a ragtag group of orphans who help Isaiah do pretty much everything.

Enemies

  • Dr Dark, a psychopathic doctor whose life goal is to capture Mind Man and steal his abilities. Believed to currently be residing somewhere in Northern Sweden.
  • The Society, a group of celestial beings who all possess Isaiah's powers. They believe he is wasting his abilities and is a failure because he does not conform to the Society's expectations.
  • X, an extremely powerful demon who has consumed all universes except ours. He is halted only by the the Society's efforts.
  • Malcolm Donovan, the corrupt leader of the World Union. He secretly hates Mind Man and wants to destroy him to maintain his control of the world.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Isaiah Thompson is an extremely dangerous individual. He has the powers of literal gods and is not to be underestimated. However, he has the support of the world on his side. He will not be easy to vanquish. I'm done. Over and out.
